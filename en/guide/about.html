Archer

Archer is a development framework based on Mahjong Soul and Tenhou. Through this framework, you can achieve communication with the game, automate game operations, and more.

Warning Using Archer "may result in account suspension", and Archer takes no responsibility for any consequences that arise. "If you care about your account", please "use a secondary account" when using this software!

Note This software does not offer a free version. If this is unacceptable, please do not download or use it. If you live in a parallel world where everything is free, or Tim Cook gifts you 10 iPhones for free each year, we might consider it. We are not philanthropists, and the cost of maintaining the software and the electricity bills for training machines are significant. We currently do not have the capacity to bear these costs.

Warning Due to the high technical knowledge required, this software may not be suitable for novices. Some users may find it difficult to understand how to use it.

AI Rankings

The highest rank Archer has achieved so far is "Tenhou 10 dan on Phoenix". Due to certain reasons, the Mahjong Soul rank "cannot be disclosed" at the moment.
