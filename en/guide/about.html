Archer

Archer is a development framework based on Mahjong Soul and Tenhou. Through this framework, you can achieve communication with the game, automate game operations, replace in-game assets, and more.

Warning Using Archer "may result in account suspension", and Archer takes no responsibility for any consequences that arise. "If you care about your account", please "use a secondary account" when using this software!

Note This software does not offer a free version. If this is unacceptable, please do not download or use it. If you live in a parallel world where everything is free, or Tim Cook gifts you 10 iPhones for free each year, we might consider it. We are not philanthropists, and the cost of maintaining the software and the electricity bills for training machines are significant. We currently do not have the capacity to bear these costs.

Warning Due to the high technical knowledge required, this software may not be suitable for novices. Some users may find it difficult to understand how to use it.

AI Rankings

The highest rank Archer has achieved so far is "9th Dan on Tenhou". Due to certain reasons, the Mahjong Soul rank "cannot be disclosed" at the moment.

Plugin Modules

Archer can "load" user-defined "plugin modules". After opening the software's "WebUI" page, you can see the "Plugin Settings" page, which displays all the loaded plugins.

Note: Some plugins require "Built-in Mode" under "Auto Settings" to be enabled for them to work. Resource replacement plugins "must be enabled" and "enter and exit the game once" for them to take effect.

At the bottom right of each plugin, there are "two important indicators":

  • "Green Shield" means the plugin is "safe".
  • "Red Exclamation Mark" means the plugin "has potential risks".

Note: The risk "only indicates a potential risk", and it does not guarantee that the plugin will result in a ban.
